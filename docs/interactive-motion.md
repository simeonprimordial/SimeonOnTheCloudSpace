# Interactive portfolio motion

The homepage uses restrained motion to improve scanning without distracting from the project content.

- Skills rotate automatically within their correct capability category.
- Hover, focus, or tap selects a skill immediately and pauses automatic rotation during interaction.
- Sections reveal once as they enter the viewport.
- Project cards, links, diagrams, and the contact panel use subtle movement and feedback.
- A reading-progress indicator appears at the top of the page.
- `prefers-reduced-motion` disables non-essential animation for accessibility.
